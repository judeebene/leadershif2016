About Leadershift 2016

LEADERSHIFT is a leadership meeting designed to build and equip emerging leaders with spiritual gifts. In a world with a dearth of genuine and authentic leaders, LEADERSHIFT will produce a new breed of leaders filled with God's spirit. Over time, emerging leaders have gone out to fulfil their callings in various fields but they have either lost their fragrance or simply got lost in the crowd. Now is the time for the Daniels and Josephs and Esthers to arise, be infused with spiritual gifts and the spirit of wisdom and excellence to bring organic spiritual leadership to our world. LEADERSHIFT aims to achieve this by bringing top leaders in diverse sectors who have a profound walk with God, who can impart necessary vision, gifts, mentoring and substance to the next generation. We believe this is a time for God's children to be adequately prepared to takeover. LEADERSHIFT is an annual event with a faculty made up of seasoned leaders in various fields across the world. In this pilot edition, LEADERSHIFT 2016, Dr Sylvanus ukafia brings to us indepth teaching laced with humour yet with spiritual substance tangibly imparted.

Speakers

Dr. Sylvanus Ukafia

senior pastor of Insight Bible Church, Uyo

Dr. Sylvanus Ukafia is a simple, pragmatic and humorous Bible teacher. He has served in numerous leadership positions and is at the vanguard of Christian leadership development in Nigeria, and indeed, Africa. In pursuit of God’s mandate to raise credible leaders, he started Insight Christian Leadership School (ICLS) in 1997, through which many pastors and market-place leaders have been trained, equipped and released to fulfill God’s call for their lives. Dr. Ukafia also pivots Pastoral leadership development program, which includes an annual Pastors Conference devoted to equipping pastors with skills to enhance their work and a series of pastors’ training sessions which holds in different parts of the country and beyond. He is also the senior pastor of Insight Bible Church, Uyo and currently the Chairman of the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria (PFN), Akwa Ibom State Chapter. Dr. Ukafia is an author of 2 books and an accomplished medical scholar with many citable publications in reputable journals to his credit. He is a member of many professional associations including the Anatomical Society of Nigeria (AAN), the Anatomical Society of West Africa (AAWA) and the Nigerian Medical Association (NMA). He is happily married to Pst. Erikan and they make their home in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, with their three children, Joy, Victory and Uziakong.

pastor Felix Alalade

Host pastor , Insight Bible church , Afaha -Oku

Alalade Felix trained in telecoms Engineering. He is a graduate of Rhema Bible Training Centre, Abuja, and a Masters Degree student in Organizational Leadership and Management at ECWA Seminary, Ibadan. He is the pastor of Insight Bible Church, Afaha Oku. He divides his time between teaching God's word and writing.
